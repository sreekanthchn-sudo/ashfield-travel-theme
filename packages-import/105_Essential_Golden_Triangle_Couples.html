Why the Golden Triangle?

The Golden Triangle is India's most iconic travel route, connecting three magnificent imperial cities that form the heartbeat of North Indian culture and heritage. This six-day couples journey unveils the splendour of the Mughal Empire, from the walled streets of Old Delhi to the marble perfection of the Taj Mahal and the palatial grandeur of Jaipur. Experience world-renowned monuments, charming bazaars, and centuries of architectural brilliance, all designed for intimate exploration and unforgettable memories.

Your Golden Triangle Journey

Day 1
DAY 1

Arrive Delhi

Arrive at Delhi Airport where you'll be warmly greeted and transferred to your hotel. Rest of the day at leisure to acclimatize and relax.

Hotel: Park Inn by Radisson, Delhi
Meals: None
Day 2
DAY 2

Delhi Sightseeing

Discover Old Delhi's cultural treasures: Red Fort, Rajghat (Gandhi's memorial), India Gate, Rashtrapati Bhawan, Humayun's Tomb, Lotus Temple, Qutub Minar, and Birla Mandir. A journey through centuries of Indian history and spirituality.

Hotel: Park Inn by Radisson, Delhi
Meals: Breakfast
Day 3
DAY 3

Delhi to Agra

Drive to Agra (210km/4hrs) in your private AC Toyota Crysta. Visit the breathtaking Taj Mahal and the imposing Agra Fort. Witness the romance and grandeur of Mughal architecture. Note: Taj Mahal is closed on Fridays.

Hotel: Royal Sarover Portico, Agra
Meals: Breakfast
Day 4
DAY 4

Agra to Jaipur via Fatehpur Sikri

Travel to Jaipur (240km/5-6hrs) with a fascinating en route stop at Fatehpur Sikri, the perfectly preserved ghost city of the Mughals. Arrive in Jaipur and explore vibrant markets by traditional cycle rickshaw in the evening.

Hotel: Nirbana Palace, Jaipur
Meals: Breakfast
Day 5
DAY 5

Jaipur Sightseeing

Explore Jaipur's iconic landmarks: Amber Fort with its grand architecture, Nahargarh Fort with panoramic views, the opulent City Palace, astronomical Jantar Mantar, the intricate Hawa Mahal, and spiritual Birla Temple. Afternoon at leisure to relax or shop.

Hotel: Nirbana Palace, Jaipur
Meals: Breakfast
Day 6
DAY 6

Departure

Drive to Delhi or Jaipur Airport (260km/5.5hrs from Jaipur if Delhi) for your onward flight. Depart with memories of India's most magnificent monuments and a deeper appreciation of Mughal heritage.

Departure: Delhi or Jaipur Airport
Meals: Breakfast

Frequently Asked Questions

What's the best time to visit the Golden Triangle?

October to March is ideal, offering pleasant temperatures (15-30°C) and clear skies perfect for sightseeing. Avoid the peak period of 15 Dec to 15 Jan due to higher costs and crowds. April to September brings intense heat and monsoon rains.

Is the Taj Mahal always open?

The Taj Mahal is closed on Fridays and on certain national holidays. Our itinerary is designed to accommodate this. Sunrise and sunset visits are particularly magical and highly recommended.

What should we wear in Delhi, Agra, and Jaipur?

During October-March, bring light layers for warm days and a light jacket for cooler evenings. Comfortable walking shoes are essential for monument exploration. Modest clothing is respectful at religious sites (shoulders and knees covered).

Is it very hot in summer?

Yes, summer (April-June) temperatures exceed 40°C, making sightseeing challenging. We recommend visiting during our listed season (October-March) for optimal comfort and experience.

Are monument entrance fees included?

No, entrance fees are not included in the base package. However, we offer a convenient add-on covering all major monument entries for INR 8,800 per person (approximately £88), which you can book when confirming your package.

What are the distances between cities?

Delhi to Agra: 210km (approximately 4 hours). Agra to Jaipur: 240km (approximately 5-6 hours, including Fatehpur Sikri stop). Jaipur to Delhi: 260km (approximately 5.5 hours). All travel is in your private AC vehicle.

Can we add a local guide?

Yes. English-speaking guides are available as an add-on at £35 per day. We recommend guides for Delhi, Agra (Taj Mahal), and Jaipur for deeper cultural insights and historical context.

What accommodation is provided?

We offer two options: Premium 4-star hotels (comfortable and well-appointed) or Luxury 5-star hotels (superior amenities and service). All include daily breakfast and are in prime locations for sightseeing.
