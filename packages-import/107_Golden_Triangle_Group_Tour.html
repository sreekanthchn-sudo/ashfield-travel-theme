Why the Golden Triangle?

The Golden Triangle is India's most iconic travel route, connecting three magnificent imperial cities that form the heartbeat of North Indian culture and heritage. This five-day group journey unveils the splendour of the Mughal Empire in the company of fellow travellers, from the palatial grandeur of Jaipur to the marble perfection of the Taj Mahal and the walled streets of Old Delhi. Experience world-renowned monuments, charming bazaars, and centuries of architectural brilliance, all designed for immersive group exploration and unforgettable shared memories aboard our luxury AC coach.

Your Golden Triangle Journey

Day 1
DAY 1

Arrive Jaipur

Arrive at Jaipur Airport where you'll be warmly greeted and transferred to your hotel via our luxury AC coach. Afternoon and evening at leisure. Jaipur sightseeing begins with visits to Amber Fort, City Palace, Jantar Mantar, Hawa Mahal, and Jal Mahal.

Day 2
DAY 2

Full Day Jaipur Sightseeing

Discover Jaipur's iconic landmarks: Jaigarh Fort with its impressive cannons, Nahargarh Fort with panoramic views, Albert Hall Museum, and Monkey Temple. Explore vibrant bazaars. Evening optional visit to Chokhi Dhani (traditional village experience) at additional cost.

Hotel: Ramada Wyndham Raja Park, Jaipur
Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner
Day 3
DAY 3

Jaipur to Agra

Drive to Agra (240km/5-6hrs) in your AC 35-seater luxury coach. Visit the breathtaking Taj Mahal and the imposing Agra Fort. Witness the romance and grandeur of Mughal architecture. Note: Taj Mahal is closed on Fridays.

Hotel: Radisson Agra
Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner
Day 4
DAY 4

Agra to Delhi

Drive to Delhi (206km/4-5hrs) in your luxury coach. Delhi sightseeing includes the magnificent Red Fort, Rajghat (Gandhi's memorial), iconic India Gate, Rashtrapati Bhawan, historic Jama Masjid, and the architectural marvel of Akshardham Temple. A journey through centuries of Indian history.

Hotel: The Metropolitan, Delhi
Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner
Day 5
DAY 5

Departure from Delhi

Transfer to Delhi Airport for your onward flight. Depart with memories of India's most magnificent monuments and a deeper appreciation of Mughal heritage shared with fellow travellers.

Departure: Delhi Airport

Frequently Asked Questions

What's the best time to visit the Golden Triangle?

October to March is ideal, offering pleasant temperatures (15-30°C) and clear skies perfect for sightseeing. This group tour specifically departs in January when weather is optimal. April to September brings intense heat and monsoon rains.

Is the Taj Mahal always open?

The Taj Mahal is closed on Fridays and on certain national holidays. Our itinerary is designed to accommodate this. Sunrise and sunset visits are particularly magical and highly recommended.

What should we wear during the tour?

January temperatures are comfortable (15-25°C). Bring light layers for warm days and a light jacket for cooler evenings. Comfortable walking shoes are essential for monument exploration. Modest clothing is respectful at religious sites (shoulders and knees covered).

How many people are in the group?

Maximum 26 guests per departure. This ensures a balanced group experience with personalized attention while maintaining group enjoyment and convenience.

Are monument entrance fees included?

No, entrance fees are not included in the base package. However, we offer a convenient add-on covering all major monument entries, which you can book when confirming your package.

What are the distances between cities?

Jaipur to Agra: 240km (approximately 5-6 hours). Agra to Delhi: 206km (approximately 4-5 hours). All travel is in your air-conditioned luxury coach with professional driver.

Is a guide included?

Yes. A professional English-speaking guide is included for all cities and sightseeing experiences, providing cultural insights and historical context throughout your journey.

What meals are provided?

All meals are included: Breakfast daily at all hotels, plus Lunch and Dinner throughout the journey. Our meal plan ensures you experience authentic Indian cuisine while maintaining comfort and dietary considerations.
