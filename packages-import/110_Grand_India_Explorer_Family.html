Why the Grand India Explorer?

The Grand India Explorer is the ultimate family adventure, combining India's most iconic northern cities with the serene backwaters of Kerala. This thirteen-day journey is thoughtfully designed to blend cultural immersion, architectural marvels, and natural beauty in a pace that suits families with children. From the bustling markets of Delhi to the peaceful houseboats of Kerala's backwaters, this adventure creates lifelong memories while introducing young minds to India's diverse heritage. Your private AC vehicle ensures comfort, flexibility, and the freedom to explore at your family's own pace.

Your Family India Journey

Days 1-2
DAY 1

Arrive Delhi

Arrive at Delhi Airport where your guide will warmly greet your family and transfer you to your hotel. Rest and acclimatize. Explore the hotel grounds and let the children settle into their new surroundings.

Hotel: Park Inn by Radisson, Delhi
Meals: None
Days 1-2
DAY 2

Delhi Sightseeing

Discover Old and New Delhi's treasures at a family-friendly pace: Red Fort, Rajghat, India Gate, Humayun's Tomb, Lotus Temple, Qutub Minar, and more. Children love the stories and grand architecture. Mix learning with play in Delhi's iconic parks and bazaars.

Hotel: Park Inn by Radisson, Delhi
Meals: Breakfast
Days 3
DAY 3

Delhi to Agra

Drive to Agra (210km/4hrs) in your private AC Toyota Crysta with plenty of stops for snacks and breaks. Visit the magnificent Taj Mahal and explore Agra Fort. Children enjoy the wonder of the ivory-white marble and the stories of Mughal emperors.

Hotel: Royal Sarover Portico, Agra
Meals: Breakfast
Days 4
DAY 4

Agra to Jaipur

Travel to Jaipur (240km/5-6hrs) with an en route stop at Fatehpur Sikri, the intriguing ghost city. Children are fascinated by the perfectly preserved Mughal architecture. Explore vibrant Jaipur bazaars by evening with time for rest and play.

Hotel: Nirbana Palace, Jaipur
Meals: Breakfast
Days 5
DAY 5

Jaipur Sightseeing

Explore Jaipur's iconic landmarks: Amber Fort (children enjoy the elephant rides and grand views), City Palace, Hawa Mahal's colorful palace, Jantar Mantar, and spiritual temples. Afternoon leisure for shopping, swimming at the hotel, or resting before travel.

Hotel: Nirbana Palace, Jaipur
Meals: Breakfast
Days 6
DAY 6

Jaipur to Cochin

Fly to Cochin and transfer to your hotel. Evening visit to a Kathakali show or explore the colonial Cochin waterfront. Children love the traditional Chinese fishing nets and vibrant local atmosphere. Settle in and enjoy local street food.

Hotel: Abad Dutch Bungalow, Cochin
Meals: Breakfast
Days 7
DAY 7

Cochin to Munnar

Drive to Munnar (140km/4hrs), a hill station famous for its sprawling tea gardens. Children enjoy the cool climate and rolling green landscapes. Explore tea plantations, visit viewpoints, and experience the tranquility of the Western Ghats.

Hotel: The Fog Resort, Munnar
Meals: Breakfast
Days 8
DAY 8

Munnar Exploration

Full day in Munnar: visit Anamudi Peak, explore Kundala Lake, wander through tea gardens, and enjoy the scenic beauty. The cool climate and peaceful surroundings make it perfect for families. Picnic lunch and nature walks at your own pace.

Hotel: The Fog Resort, Munnar
Meals: Breakfast
Days 9
DAY 9

Munnar to Kumarakom

Drive to Kumarakom (150km/4hrs) on the banks of Vembanad Lake. Visit the famous bird sanctuary where children spot migratory birds and rare species. Sunset walks along the shore are magical for families. Enjoy the peaceful backwater setting.

Hotel: Lake Song Resort, Kumarakom
Meals: Breakfast
Days 10
DAY 10

Alleppey Backwater Cruise & Marari Beach

Experience a magical houseboat lunch cruise through Alleppey's palm-fringed backwaters. Children delight in the peaceful waterways, spotting fish and birds. Afternoon transfer to Marari Beach. This is the highlight for most families—a truly unique experience.

Hotel: Abad Turtle Beach Resort, Marari Beach
Meals: Breakfast, Lunch
Days 11
DAY 11

Marari Beach Leisure

Full day at the pristine Marari Beach resort. Children play safely on the beach, swim in the shallow waters, and enjoy water sports. The resort offers relaxation for parents while kids explore tide pools, build sandcastles, and learn about coastal ecosystems.

Hotel: Abad Turtle Beach Resort, Marari Beach
Meals: Breakfast
Days 12
DAY 12

Marari to Cochin

Drive back to Cochin (70km/2hrs) with leisure time for last-minute shopping in Fort Cochin's charming spice markets and boutiques. Visit local artisan shops or relax at a café. Children enjoy the colonial atmosphere and vibrant street culture.

Hotel: Abad Dutch Bungalow, Cochin
Meals: Breakfast
Days 13
DAY 13

Departure

Depart from Cochin Airport for your onward flight. Depart with memories of India's cultural diversity, natural beauty, and the special moments shared as a family—truly an unforgettable journey.

Departure: Cochin Airport
Meals: Breakfast

India Visa Information

Visa Requirements

Most international visitors require a visa to enter India. Citizens of select countries are eligible for e-Visa, which can be obtained online—convenient for families traveling with children.

Tourist Visa (e-Visa)

Valid for 30 or 60 days from the date of issue. Multiple entries available. Easy online application through India's official e-Visa portal, perfect for family travel planning.

Processing Time

e-Visa typically approved within 3-4 business days. We recommend applying at least 2 weeks before your departure date. Children need their own visas.

For official visa information and eligibility, visit the India e-Visa Official Portal

Family Travel FAQs

Is this package suitable for children of all ages?

Yes! We recommend this package for children ages 5-14. Younger children (2-4) are welcome but may find long drives tiring. Children under 2 travel free. We can adjust the itinerary for younger families with more rest days and shorter driving distances.

What's the best time to visit with children?

October to March is ideal with pleasant temperatures (15-30°C) perfect for families. Avoid peak 15 Dec – 15 Jan for fewer crowds. April to September brings intense heat unsuitable for children. Monsoon (June-September) is not recommended for family travel.

How long are the daily drives?

Longest drives are 4-6 hours (Delhi-Agra, Agra-Jaipur, Cochin-Munnar). We include multiple stops for snacks, play, and bathroom breaks. Your private AC vehicle ensures comfort. Shorter 1-4 hour drives in Kerala leg.

Are children's meals provided?

Breakfast is included daily at all hotels with kid-friendly options. Lunch and dinner are not included, allowing flexibility for family preferences. Hotels and restaurants offer Indian, continental, and familiar cuisines suitable for children.

What activities are best for children?

Children love the Taj Mahal, Amber Fort elephant rides, tea gardens walks, bird watching, beach play at Marari, and the houseboat cruise. Each destination offers learning through play. We can add activities like swimming, cooking classes, or nature walks.

Is the Kerala houseboat safe for children?

Yes, absolutely! Houseboats are safe with railings and trained staff. Children love the experience of gliding through backwaters, spotting birds, and enjoying peaceful water. It's often the highlight of family trips!

What health precautions should we take?

Consult your doctor about vaccinations 4-6 weeks before travel. Pack high SPF sunscreen for children, insect repellent, and basic medications. Drink boiled/bottled water. All our hotels maintain high hygiene standards. Travel insurance covering medical evacuation is recommended.

Can we customize the itinerary for our family?

Absolutely! We offer flexible itineraries tailored to your family's pace. Want longer beach time? Skip a monument? Add rest days? We can adjust. Contact us to discuss your family's specific needs and preferences.

What clothing should children wear?

Light, breathable clothing for warm days, with a light jacket for cooler evenings and hill stations. Comfortable walking shoes are essential. At religious sites, cover shoulders and knees (modest clothing). Sun hats and sunglasses protect from strong sun.

Are monument entrance fees included?

No, entrance fees are not included in the base package. However, we offer a convenient add-on covering all major monuments for families. Children often receive discounted entry. Ask us for current pricing and availability.
