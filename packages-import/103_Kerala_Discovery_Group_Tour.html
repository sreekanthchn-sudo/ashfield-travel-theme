Why Kerala Group Tours?

Kerala Discovery is a carefully curated escorted group tour designed for travellers who want the magic of Kerala without the hassle of solo planning. With a maximum of 26 guests, this journey offers the perfect balance: the camaraderie and shared experiences of group travel, combined with the attentiveness of professional guides and the flexibility to explore at your own pace. From the historic charm of Fort Cochin to the serene Alleppey Backwaters and the golden beaches of Kovalam, this 7-day adventure showcases God's Own Country in all its splendour.

Our escorted groups benefit from an experienced tour leader, AC luxury coach transport, daily breakfast and meals included, and carefully selected 4-star hotels (with 5-star options available). Every aspect is arranged—sightseeing, transfers, most meals, entrance fees—so you simply arrive, relax, and make memories with fellow travellers. Whether spotting exotic birds at Kumarakom, cruising the backwaters on a private houseboat, or watching the sunset at Kovalam Beach, Kerala Discovery transforms you into an informed, confident explorer with new friends at your side.

Your Day-by-Day Journey

Arrival in Cochin
DAY 1

Arrive in Cochin & Explore Fort Cochin

Welcome to Kerala! Meet your tour group and guide at Cochin Airport and transfer to your hotel in Fort Cochin. After settling in, embark on a leisurely exploration of this charming historic quarter. Wander the colonial streets, visit the iconic Chinese Fishing Nets, discover local spice markets, and soak in the atmosphere of this UNESCO World Heritage site. In the evening, gather with your group for a traditional dinner, marking the beginning of your Kerala adventure.

Hotel: Old Court House, Cochin
Meals: Dinner
Cochin Sightseeing & Drive to Munnar
DAY 2

Cochin Sightseeing & Drive to Munnar

After breakfast, your guide leads a comprehensive sightseeing tour of Cochin. Visit the Dutch Palace (Mattancherry Palace) with its ancient murals, the centuries-old Jewish Synagogue surrounded by spice markets, the historic St Francis Church, and more. Enjoy lunch at a local restaurant before boarding your AC luxury coach for the scenic drive to Munnar (140 km, approximately 4 hours). Wind through plantation-covered hills and waterfall-studded landscapes. Arrive in the cool hill station and settle into your resort for the evening.

Hotel: Edan Woods Resort, Munnar
Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner
Distance: 140 km / 4 hrs
Munnar Tea Plantations
DAY 3

Munnar Sightseeing

Spend a full day exploring the breathtaking beauty of Munnar with your English-speaking guide. Visit the picturesque Mattupetty Dam, experience panoramic views from Echo Point, climb to Rajamala—home to the endangered Nilgiri Tahr—and tour a working spice garden to learn about cardamom, pepper, and clove cultivation at 1,600 metres above sea level. Lunch at a local restaurant provides authentic flavours and a chance for group conversation. Return to your resort as the misty evening descends, with time to relax and enjoy the cool mountain air.

Hotel: Edan Woods Resort, Munnar
Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner
Kumarakom Lake & Bird Sanctuary
DAY 4

Drive to Kumarakom & Bird Sanctuary

After breakfast, board your coach for the drive to Kumarakom (150 km, approximately 4 hours), a serene destination nestled on the banks of Vembanad Lake—Kerala's biggest and longest freshwater lake. Check into your lakeside resort and spend the afternoon exploring the Kumarakom Bird Sanctuary with your guide. Over 200 species of migratory and resident birds inhabit this paradise, including Siberian cranes, herons, egrets, and kingfishers. Spot them from viewing platforms as the golden afternoon light dances across the water. Group lunch and dinner allow you to bond with fellow travellers.

Hotel: Lake Song Resort, Kumarakom
Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner
Distance: 150 km / 4 hrs
Alleppey Houseboat Day Cruise
DAY 5

Alleppey Day Cruise & Transfer to Marari Beach

After breakfast, transfer to Alleppey (50 km, approximately 1.5 hours), the gateway to Kerala's famous backwaters. Board your private air-conditioned houseboat for an unforgettable day cruise through the intricate network of lagoons, lakes, and canals. Drift past coconut palms, spice plantations, and traditional fishing villages. A sumptuous lunch, prepared with fresh seafood and authentic Kerala cuisine, is served on board—a magical moment shared with your group. In the late afternoon, transfer to Marari Beach Resort (30 km, approximately 1 hour) and settle into your beachfront accommodation as the sun dips below the horizon.

Hotel: Abad Turtle Beach Resort, Marari
Meals: Breakfast, Lunch (on houseboat), Dinner
Distance: 50 km + 30 km
Marari to Kovalam via Trivandrum Heritage
DAY 6

Drive to Kovalam & Trivandrum Sightseeing

After breakfast, board your coach for the journey to Kovalam (160 km, approximately 4 hours). En route, stop in Trivandrum (the capital) for a guided heritage sightseeing tour. Visit the striking Padmanabhaswamy Temple, one of the world's richest temples with its iconic gopuram, the historic Napier Museum with its eclectic art collections, and the Government Art Gallery. Enjoy lunch at a local restaurant. Arrive at Kovalam in the late afternoon and check into your beachfront resort. Spend the rest of the day at leisure on the iconic crescent beach or relax at your resort.

Hotel: Uday Samudra Beach & Leisure Resort, Kovalam
Meals: Breakfast, Lunch, Dinner
Distance: 160 km / 4 hrs
Departure from Trivandrum
DAY 7

Transfer to Trivandrum Airport & Departure

After breakfast, transfer to Trivandrum International Airport (approximately 1 hour from Kovalam) for your onward flight. As you fly home, reflect on an unforgettable week exploring God's Own Country. The memories of mist-covered mountains, serene backwaters, pristine beaches, and the friendships forged with your group will stay with you long after your return home.

Meals: Breakfast

Indian Visa Information

Who Does This Apply To?

British passport holders can apply for an Indian e-Visa online, which is the most convenient option for UK citizens travelling to India for tourism purposes. The e-Visa is a digital visa issued electronically and does not require a physical stamp in your passport.

How to Apply

Visit indianvisaonline.gov.in/evisa/tvoa.html to apply for your Indian e-Visa. Apply no earlier than 30 days before your arrival date; we recommend applying at least 28 days in advance. Ensure your passport is valid for at least 6 months from your travel date and has at least 2 blank pages.

Indian Reference Contact

When applying for your e-Visa, you may be asked for an Indian reference contact. If needed, please contact us and we can assist with providing the necessary details for your application.

Need Help with Your Visa?

While the online e-Visa process is straightforward, professional visa assistance is available through The Travel Visa Company. Please note that third-party visa fees apply if you choose this service. We recommend applying yourself first, as it is usually quicker and more cost-effective.

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the group size for this tour?

Kerala Discovery is limited to a maximum of 26 guests, ensuring a personalised experience while enjoying the benefits of group travel. The minimum group size is 15 guests. This carefully calibrated size allows you to meet fellow travellers and benefit from group facilities while still maintaining an intimate, manageable atmosphere.

What is the coach like, and how comfortable are the journeys?

Your group travels in an AC luxury 34-seater coach with a professional driver. The coach is fitted with comfortable seating, air conditioning, and other amenities to ensure pleasant journeys between destinations. While some journeys (such as Cochin to Munnar) span 4 hours, the scenic routes, regular rest stops, and the comfort of the coach make these drives enjoyable rather than taxing.

Can you accommodate dietary requirements?

Yes, we can accommodate most dietary requirements including vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and other restrictions. Please inform us of any dietary requirements at the time of booking so we can notify the hotels and coordinate with the tour leader. Indian cuisine naturally includes excellent vegetarian options, and our hotel partners are experienced in catering to diverse dietary needs.

Are there single rooms available, and what is the single supplement?

While the package is based on twin/double occupancy, single rooms are available at a supplement. Please contact us for current single supplement pricing. Availability of single rooms is limited, so we recommend booking early if you require one.

What is the age range of typical group members?

Our Kerala Discovery tours attract travellers of all ages—from younger independent travellers to couples and retirees. Most groups include a healthy mix of ages and backgrounds, all united by a passion for exploring Kerala. The group tour format appeals to solo travellers looking for companionship, friends travelling together, and couples wanting organised, hassle-free holidays.

Do I need a visa to travel to India?

Yes, British passport holders require a visa to enter India. The most convenient option is an e-Visa, which can be applied for online at indianvisaonline.gov.in/evisa/tvoa.html. The process is straightforward and typically takes 4-5 working days. We recommend applying at least 28 days before your arrival date.

What should I pack for a Kerala group tour?

Pack lightweight, breathable clothing suitable for a tropical climate. Include comfortable walking shoes, sandals, a light rain jacket (if travelling during shoulder seasons), sunscreen, sunglasses, and a hat. For temple visits, carry a shawl or lightweight scarf to cover shoulders and knees. Swimming attire is essential for beach days. In Munnar, where temperatures are cooler, bring a light cardigan for evenings.

What is the best time to travel with Kerala Discovery?

Kerala Discovery operates in January, when the weather is cool, dry, and perfect for sightseeing. This is the peak tourist season, and the climate is ideal for outdoor activities and exploration. Specific departure dates are available upon request. The Southwest Monsoon (June-September) is avoided, as heavy rainfall can disrupt travel plans.

Are flights included in the package price?

No, this is a land-only package and international flights are not included. You'll arrive at Cochin Airport on Day 1 and depart from Trivandrum Airport on Day 7. We recommend booking your flights separately and arranging your own travel insurance. We can provide guidance on flight recommendations and arrange airport transfers as part of your package.

What happens if I need to cancel or make changes?

We understand that circumstances change. Please contact us to discuss cancellation and amendment policies, which vary depending on how far in advance you cancel. Travel insurance is highly recommended to protect yourself against unforeseen circumstances. Our team will work with you to find the best solution.
