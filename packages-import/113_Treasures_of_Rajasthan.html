Why Rajasthan?

Rajasthan is India's most enchanting state, a land of majestic forts, ornate palaces, and vibrant culture that transports you back to the reign of warrior kings and emperors. This six-day journey through the Golden Triangle of Rajasthan connects three spectacular cities that embody the romance of the Thar Desert and the architectural splendor of centuries past. From the hilltop fortress of Jodhpur to the golden dunes of Jaisalmer, experience a remarkable blend of history, heritage, and desert adventure designed to create unforgettable memories.

Your Rajasthan Journey

Day 1
DAY 1

Arrive Jaipur

Arrive in Jaipur and check in to your hotel. Spend the afternoon exploring the city's iconic landmarks: Albert Hall, Hawa Mahal, Jantar Mantar astronomical site, City Palace, Govind Dev Ji Temple, and vibrant local markets showcasing traditional Rajasthani crafts.

Hotel: To be confirmed
Meals: None
Day 2
DAY 2

Jaipur Full Day Exploration

Full day dedicated to Jaipur's magnificent forts and temples. Visit the grand Amber Fort with its palatial rooms and intricate artwork, Jaigarh Fort known for its cannons, Nahargarh Fort with breathtaking city views, Jal Mahal reflecting in its lake, and the spiritual Birla Temple with its marble carvings.

Hotel: Jaipur hotel
Meals: Breakfast
Day 3
DAY 3

Drive to Jodhpur

Journey by private vehicle to Jodhpur, the "Blue City." Explore the magnificent Mehrangarh Fort, a hilltop fortress visible from across the city with stunning architecture and museum exhibits. Visit the beautiful white marble Jaswant Thada cenotaph, serene Balsamand Lake, and explore Ghanta Ghar market for traditional handicrafts and textiles.

Hotel: Jodhpur hotel
Meals: Breakfast
Day 4
DAY 4

Drive to Jaisalmer - The Golden City

Drive to Jaisalmer, known as the "Golden City" for its stunning sandstone architecture. Explore the Jaisalmer War Memorial, the magnificent living Jaisalmer Fort rising from the desert, and intricate havelis including Patwon ki Haveli, Nathmal ki Haveli, and Salim Singh ki Haveli with their elaborate 12th-15th century carvings. Browse leather goods and traditional crafts in local markets.

Hotel: Jaisalmer hotel
Meals: Breakfast
Day 5
DAY 5

Desert Adventure and Desert Camp

Visit the historic Tanot Mata shrine and the war memorial at Longewala. In the afternoon, head to Sam Sand Dunes for an authentic desert experience. Enjoy dune bashing in a 4WD, camel rides at sunset, traditional folk performances, a roaring bonfire, and a delicious Rajasthani meal under the stars. Overnight at a comfortable desert camp.

Hotel: Desert Camp, Jaisalmer
Meals: Breakfast, Dinner
Day 6
DAY 6

Departure

After breakfast, you will be transferred to Jodhpur Airport or Jaisalmer Airport for your onward journey. Depart with unforgettable memories of Rajasthan's magnificent forts, palaces, and desert landscapes.

Departure: Jodhpur or Jaisalmer Airport
Meals: Breakfast

Frequently Asked Questions

What is the best time to visit Rajasthan?

October to March is ideal, offering pleasant temperatures (15-28°C) and clear skies perfect for sightseeing and desert exploration. Avoid April-September when temperatures exceed 40°C and monsoon rains can affect travel.

How hot is the Rajasthan desert?

During the recommended October-March season, daytime temperatures range from 15-25°C, while evenings can be cool (8-15°C). The desert camp provides blankets and warm clothing for comfort. Summer months (April-September) can exceed 40°C, making that season unsuitable for travel.

What should I wear in Rajasthan?

Pack light, breathable clothing for warm days and a warm jacket or shawl for cool evenings and nights in the desert. Comfortable walking shoes are essential for fort and temple exploration. Modest clothing (shoulders and knees covered) is respectful at religious sites. A hat and sunscreen are recommended.

Is the desert camp comfortable?

Yes, our desert camp offers authentic yet comfortable accommodation with traditional tents, quality bedding, and proper facilities. While it's a unique experience under the stars, standards ensure your comfort and safety. It's an unforgettable highlight of the journey.

Can I ride a camel?

Yes, camel rides at sunset in Sam Sand Dunes are a magical highlight of Day 5. We take all safety precautions, and our experienced guides ensure a memorable experience. The rides are suitable for most fitness levels.

Are monument entrance fees included?

Monument entrance fees are not included in the base package but can be arranged as an add-on. Please contact us for current fee details and we can arrange them as part of your booking.

What are the travel distances?

Jaipur to Jodhpur: approximately 350km (6-7 hours). Jodhpur to Jaisalmer: approximately 280km (5-6 hours). All travel is by private vehicle with rest breaks included for your comfort and safety.

Can a guide be added?

Yes, English-speaking local guides are available as an add-on service. We recommend guides for fort visits and temple sites to enhance cultural insights and historical understanding of these magnificent monuments.
